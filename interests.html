Welcome to my Interests!

A little corner of my brain where I nerd out about anime, drown myself in the work of books, and reset myself with hobbies.

Anime

From absolute peak fiction such as Attack On Titan to deep psychologial shows like Death Note, anime has been my escape from the stresses of life since way back in 6th grade. I love the stunning art styles, insane character development, and lore that gets you thinking. My current and longest hyperfixation has been Blue Lock with its immensely motivational messages.

Other HMs: Solo Leveling, Free!, The Summer Hikaru Died

Books

Nothing compares to the smell of fresh pages. I love love love reading. From the magical world of Harry Potter to the sci-fi filled mystery that is Warcross. A series I was OBSESSED with as a child that will never leave my heart called The Unwanteds has such amazing world building. With school and all I haven't had much time to read these past few months but I am getting back on track with Maze Runner as the movie did it justice.

"A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies. The man who never reads lives only one." - GEORGE R.R. MARTIN
